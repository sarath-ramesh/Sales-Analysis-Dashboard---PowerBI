## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Sales Analysis Dashboard** built using Power BI, based on the Superstore dataset (Excel source). The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into sales performance, profit distribution, and order trends to support **data-driven business decisions**.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing segments and categories
* Track sales trends across regions and time
* Enable interactive exploration of business data

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 🔹 KPI Cards

* **Total Orders:** 5009
* **Total Sales:** $2.30M
* **Total Profit:** $286.40K

👉 Provides a quick overview of overall business performance.

---

### 🔹 Segment-wise Analysis

* Consumer (Highest contribution)
* Corporate
* Home Office

**Insight:**
Consumer segment drives the majority of sales and profit.

---

### 🔹 Category-wise Sales

* Technology (Highest)
* Furniture
* Office Supplies

**Insight:**
Technology category is the leading revenue generator.

---

### 🔹 Sales by Person

* Individual sales contribution visualized using donut chart

**Insight:**
Helps identify top-performing sales representatives.

---

### 🔹 Segment-wise Profit

* Consumer: Highest profit
* Corporate: Moderate
* Home Office: Lowest

**Insight:**
Profit distribution aligns with sales contribution across segments.

---

### 🔹 State-wise Sales (Map Visualization)

* Geographic representation of sales across regions

**Insight:**
Highlights high-performing states and regional sales distribution.

---

### 🔹 Filters & Interactivity

* Subcategory filter
* Order Date range filter

👉 Enables dynamic and customized data exploration.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Consumer segment dominates both sales and profit
* Technology is the most profitable category
* Strong regional variations in sales performance
* Sales performance varies significantly across sales representatives
* Overall business shows healthy profitability
